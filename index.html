Biography

I am currently a third-year PhD student in City University of Hong Kong, co-supervised by Cornell University’s College of Veterinary Medicine. Before that, I got my master's degree at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, and I attained my bachelor's degree from Shenzhen University. My research interests include bioinformatics, metagenome and precision livestock farming.
